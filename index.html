About Me

I am Viraj. A Master of Information Systems student at Pace University and an aspiring Product Manager


From Engineering to Product Management

With a background in ETC, my journey began with an interest in understanding how systems work at a core level. I took this knowledge further during my work as an Data Analyst & also Software Engineer where I did code of course! & analyzed datasets. My passion for solving complex tech problems, combined with my hands on experience, naturally guided me towards management

My passion for management

While I appreciate coding and the logic behind it, I realized early on that it wasn't my true calling. What truly drives me is the excitement of tackling problems and finding ingenious solutions. To me, management is an extension of this problem solving mindset, with an added layer of creativity

Transitioning to Design & Beyond

I've learned a lot from my current major and previous roles, and it has really influenced how I approach design. Making me good at picking up new ideas and solving problems, even ones that are outside of my expertise. I also have a knack for storytelling, which helps me explain the vision and purpose behind each project

Problem Solving as a Core Philosophy

Managing a product is more than just checking off tasks; it's about creating a story and seeing it through to a successful ending. I think every project has its own unique tale, and my job is to make sure it's both interesting and successful

So why Viraj?

I am someone who confidently manages & guides teams while remaining open to new ideas. I value thorough research & do not pursue tasks without purpose. I handle situations calmly & empathetically. Organized & methodical in my daily life, excelling in predictive analysis with practical applications. So yes, that's me

Beyond Work

When I'm not busy with design or technical stuff, you'll usually find me traveling, taking photos, or getting into a new FIFA challenge. I absolutely hate losing, and I love watching Manchester United play. These activities not only help me recharge but also inspire my creativity, patience, and belief and keep me curious
